Following are the rules in This monopoly game :  

1. Player 1 will be represented as P1 (purple) on the monopoly board.

2. Player 2 will be represented as P2 (green) on the monopoly board.

3. Players can buy the property after landing on the location that wants to buy.

4. If they landed on (Go to Jail or Jail) 500 rupees will be automatically deducted from their balance.

5. If player land on a property owned by another player, the listed amount of rent will be deducted from that player.  

6. Balance will be shown at the start of each turn and can also be seen from the menu.

7. The first player to reach 0 balance will be declared bankrupt and will be the loser of the game.
